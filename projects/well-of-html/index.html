Here at slowcooker recipe land we are dedicated too compiling a list of some of the greatest slowcooker recipes we can find. We are accepting any new recipes through the form below so please submit any you feel like sharing

We recommend only using fresh bought ingrediants as the consistency and times may change depending on them.

  • Carrots
  • Beef Roast
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Beef Stock
  • Potatoes
  • French Onion Soup Mix
  • Onions
  1. Flour and salt beef roast then sear
  2. Cook onions in butter until translucent
  3. Add cream of mushroom soup and french onion soup mix to pot
  4. Add beef stock and onions, mix well
  5. Add beef roast
  6. Cut carrots and potatoes and add on top
  7. Slow cook for 4 hours
Searing
When searing be careful that no water is left on whatever meat you are searing
Tips
Do not leave the slowcooker on for more than several hours after it's finished
